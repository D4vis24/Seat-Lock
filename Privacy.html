Introduction

SeatLock ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information with third parties.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Practice Preferences: Target airline selection, interview date, and daily goals
  • Practice Data: Your responses to practice questions, scores, and progress tracking
  • Voice Data: When using voice practice features, audio is processed locally on your device

Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: App feature usage, session duration, and practice statistics (stored locally only)
  • Device Information: Device type and OS version for compatibility

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized interview practice experience
  • Track your progress and provide performance insights
  • Generate AI-powered feedback on your practice responses
  • Improve app features and user experience

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All practice data, preferences, and progress are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Cloud Processing: When using AI grading, your responses are sent securely for analysis but are NOT stored after processing

Third-Party Services

AI Grading (Claude API)

We use Anthropic's Claude API for AI-powered feedback. Your responses are:

  • Transmitted securely via HTTPS
  • Not stored by Anthropic after processing
  • Used only to generate feedback for your session

Apple Services

  • Speech Recognition: Processed using Apple's on-device speech recognition
  • App Store: Purchases processed through Apple's App Store

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing
  • Access your responses for any purpose other than providing the service

Your Rights

  • Delete Your Data: Use "Reset All Progress" in Settings to delete all local data
  • Export Your Data: Contact us for a copy of your data
  • Opt Out: Disable specific features like voice practice at any time

Security

  • HTTPS encryption for all network communications
  • Local data encryption using iOS security features
  • No storage of sensitive credentials on our servers

Children's Privacy

SeatLock is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
